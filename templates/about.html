My Story

My love for programming began in 9th grade with a simple "Hello, World!" in C—that tiny program ignited a passion for turning logic into creation. By 12th grade, I dove into web development, crafting my first pages with HTML, CSS, and Java, and I was hooked.

Recently, I’ve expanded into app development, focusing on intuitive, user-friendly experiences.

I thrive in problem-solving and am fluent in C, Java, Python, and JavaScript, with a deep focus on mastering Java. Every day, I’m learning something new—whether it’s a framework, a design pattern, or a better way to debug life’s errors.
